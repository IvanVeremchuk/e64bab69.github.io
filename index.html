American Council on Exercise

The American Council on Exercise (ACE) is a leading nonprofit organization with over 90,000 well-respected certified exercise professionals and health coaches worldwide who share a mission to get people moving. Since 1985, ACE has been working to significantly impact inactivity-related diseases by offering NCCA-accredited certifications, publishing original research, convening experts on physical activity and health, working directly with community groups and advocating for policies to help people from all walks of life gain access to the resources they need to lead active, healthy lives.
